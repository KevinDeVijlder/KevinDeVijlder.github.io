About

I'm Kevin, also known as Owly, a cybersecurity specialist with a passion for understanding how systems work, identifying weaknesses, and helping make the digital world a safer place. My interests span across offensive and defensive security, continuous learning, and staying up to date with the ever-evolving cybersecurity landscape.

Outside of my professional work, I actively invest time in developing my skills through hands-on labs and practical challenges. I regularly use platforms such as TryHackMe and Hack The Box to sharpen my technical abilities, explore new attack techniques, and gain deeper insight into real-world security scenarios. I also enjoy documenting my learning journey by publishing write-ups and technical articles on Medium, where I share lessons learned, walkthroughs, and cybersecurity insights with the broader community.

I believe that cybersecurity is a field where curiosity and continuous improvement are essential. Whether I'm researching a new vulnerability, building a home lab, or experimenting with security tools and technologies, I'm always looking for opportunities to learn something new and expand my knowledge.

When I'm away from the keyboard, you'll usually find me exploring nature on a hiking trail, enjoying movies and TV shows on my home theater setup, or experimenting in the kitchen. I have a particular appreciation for good food and enjoy cooking meals from scratch.
